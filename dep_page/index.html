Vision of CSE Department

The department strives to produce competent and qualified computer professionals and researchers to serve the community and profession with moral values and ethics.

Mission of CSE Department

  1. To impart professional education emphasizing intellectual ability creation
  2. To provide learning ambience for enhancing innovations, research, and values among the students
  3. To collaborate with industries for giving opportunities to students to develop their employability and entrepreneurship skills
  4. To inculcate professional behavior, positive attitude, and communication skills
